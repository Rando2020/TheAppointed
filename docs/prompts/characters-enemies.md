# Enemy Character Prompts

This document provides prompts to generate idle and attack poses for three enemy creatures.  The sprites should be detailed pixel art that fits the game’s darker, mature tone.  Each enemy also requires two versions: an idle animation frame and an attack animation frame.

## Null Drake
*A malformed draconic beast birthed from void magic.  Its scales are a dull black with purple undertones, and arcs of swirling energy radiate from its spine.*

- **Idle Pose:** *The Null Drake crouches low to the ground, wings partially folded, its tail coiled around its body.  Wisps of purple energy leak between the scales.*
- **Attack Pose:** *The creature lunges forward with jaws open wide, a stream of dark energy building in its throat.  The wings flare out for balance, emphasizing its feral power.*

## Storm Imp
*A mischievous elemental imp crackling with electricity.  Its skin is cobalt blue, and tiny sparks continuously dance across its body.  It has small horns and a long, thin tail.*

- **Idle Pose:** *The Storm Imp hovers just above the ground with arms crossed, electricity arcing between its fingers.  Its expression is cocky.*
- **Attack Pose:** *The imp thrusts its hands forward, releasing a concentrated bolt of lightning.  Its body arches backward as the power surges.*

## Fen Wraith
*A spectral creature that haunts marshlands.  It appears as a semi-transparent humanoid wrapped in tattered robes of algae and moss.  Its face is a skull partially covered in reeds.*

- **Idle Pose:** *The Fen Wraith floats with its robes drifting slowly as if underwater.  Its skeletal hands clutch a rusty lantern that emits a faint green glow.*
- **Attack Pose:** *The wraith lunges, extending one arm as wisps of swamp gas coalesce into a spear that it thrusts forward.  Its lantern flashes brightly, illuminating its hollow eyes.*
