# Player Unit Character Prompts

This file defines image generation prompts for our three playable characters.  Each character requires two full-body sprites (idle and action) and a square portrait crop for use in UI panels.  The style should be detailed pixel art with a grounded, mature feel reminiscent of late-90s tactical RPGs.

## Zane
Zane is a disciplined swordsman from a remote mountain village.  He wears layered leather and metal armor, with a flowing dark red scarf.  His hair is short and black with a single white streak.

- **Idle Pose:** *Zane stands with one hand resting on the pommel of his sheathed sword.  His posture is calm but alert, feet shoulder-width apart.  Subtle cloth folds and armor detail convey realism.*
- **Action Pose:** *Zane is mid-slash, sword drawn in a wide horizontal arc.  His body leans into the attack, scarf trailing behind him to emphasize motion.*
- **Portrait Crop:** *A square head-and-shoulders portrait of Zane looking directly at the viewer with a serious expression.  The background is a simple gradient to match the UI tone.*

## Mira
Mira is a quick-witted archer and scout.  She wears light leather armor with green accents and a hooded cloak.  Her hair is dark brown, pulled back into a short braid.

- **Idle Pose:** *Mira stands casually with her bow slung over one shoulder and a hand on her hip.  Her expression is confident and slightly mischievous.*
- **Action Pose:** *Mira is in a dynamic shooting stance, bow drawn and arrow nocked, aiming toward an unseen target.  Her cloak flares out to show motion.*
- **Portrait Crop:** *A square portrait of Mira smiling subtly, hood pulled back to reveal her braid.  Highlight her keen eyes and agile features.*

## Kael
Kael is a scholarly mage devoted to ancient lore.  He wears layered robes with intricate patterns and carries a staff topped with a crystal.  His hair is silver and shoulder-length.

- **Idle Pose:** *Kael stands upright, hands clasped over his staff.  His expression is contemplative and serene, and his robes flow naturally around his feet.*
- **Action Pose:** *Kael extends his staff forward as runes swirl around the crystal, casting a spell.  His robes billow slightly, hinting at magical energy.*
- **Portrait Crop:** *A square portrait of Kael gazing thoughtfully to the side, the top of his staff visible in the frame.  Soft lighting emphasizes his wisdom.*
