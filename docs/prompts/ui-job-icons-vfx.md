# Job Icons & Visual Effects Prompts

This document describes prompts for generating iconography and visual effects used in the game’s job system and combat feedback.  Keep the style consistent with the game’s darker medieval-fantasy tone.

## Job Class Icons
Create small, emblematic icons representing each of the six starting classes.  Each icon should be contained within a circular or hexagonal badge and rely on clear silhouettes.

1. **Knight Icon:** A stylized helmet with a raised visor and plume, rendered in silver with a dark background.
2. **Mage Icon:** A crystal orb held by curling flames or energy, suggesting arcane power.
3. **Cleric Icon:** A pair of crossed staves or a sacred symbol with radiant lines, implying healing and protection.
4. **Rogue Icon:** A shadowy dagger or hooded silhouette, evoking stealth and agility.
5. **Archer Icon:** A bow with an arrow drawn, framed by a crescent shape to suggest precision.
6. **Guardian Icon:** A sturdy shield emblazoned with a lion or mythical beast, denoting defense and resilience.

## Elemental Impact VFX
Generate animated visual effects for five elemental impacts.  These should be rendered as effects layers to overlay on attack frames.

1. **Fire Impact:** A burst of flames with bright orange and yellow tongues and sparking embers.
2. **Ice Impact:** Shattering shards of ice accompanied by a cold mist, tinted blue and white.
3. **Lightning Impact:** Jagged bolts of electricity crackling outward in a strobing pattern.
4. **Earth Impact:** A localized shockwave of dust and small rocks erupting from the point of impact.
5. **Wind Impact:** Swirling gusts and translucent slicing arcs, bending around the target.

## Damage Number Floats
Design floating damage numbers that appear above units when they take damage.  Use a chunky pixel font with shading to separate the numbers from the background.  Provide color variations for standard damage (white), critical hits (yellow), and healing (green).

## Guardian Summon Illustrations
Create two half-body illustrations for Guardian summons.  These should be detailed concept art pieces used in the summoning animation and on ability cards.

1. **Titan Guardian:** A colossal stone colossus with mossy growths and glowing runes etched into its body.  It holds a massive hammer and exudes ancient strength.
2. **Siren Guardian:** A graceful water spirit with long flowing hair that transitions into waves.  Her lower body is made of swirling water, and she carries a trident adorned with seashell motifs.
